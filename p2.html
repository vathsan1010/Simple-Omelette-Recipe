Simple Omelette Recipe

With a few basic steps and a flip of the wrist, you can pull off a perfect homemade omelette in just a few minutes.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 Tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling:cheese,herbs,etc

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add a small pinch of salt and beat the eggs with a fork until they are well combined.
  2. Heat the pan:In an 8-inch nonstick skillet over medium-low heat, melt the butter.
  3. Cook the omelette:Add the eggs to the skillet and cook without stirring until the edges begin to set. With a silicone spatula.
  4. Add fillings:Place the cheese, tomatoes, and herbs in a line down the center of the omelette and cook for about 1 minute longer
  5. Fold and serve:Slide the spatula around one side of the omelette at the edge to loosen it. Slip it under the eggs, and use it to carefully fold the omelette in half.
  6. Enjoy:Tilt the pan over a plate and use the spatula to nudge it onto the plate
